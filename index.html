Privacy Policy – Weekly360

Data Storage
All meals, nutrition data, weight entries, water intake, and glucose logs are stored locally on your device. Weekly360 does not create user accounts and does not maintain a remote database of personal data.

AI Food Analysis
When using camera, photo library, or text analysis features, food images or descriptions may be processed by a third-party AI service to generate nutrition estimates. No personal identity information is intentionally collected or stored.

Health Data
If permission is granted, Weekly360 may access Apple Health data (such as calories burned). This data is used only within the app and is never sold or shared externally.

Advertising
The free version of Weekly360 uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect anonymized device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases
Premium subscriptions are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

Data Collection & Sharing
Weekly360 does not sell, trade, or share personal user data.

Privacy Policy – MyScorePaper

MyScorePaper does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on external servers. All scores, player names, and game history remain stored locally on the device unless explicitly stated within the app.

Advertising
The free version of MyScorePaper uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect anonymized device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

MyScorePaper does not require account creation and does not sell or share user data.
